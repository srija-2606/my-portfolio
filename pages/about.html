My Story

Hi! I'm Alex — a full-stack developer and designer passionate about building products that are as accessible as they are beautiful. I believe the web should work for everyone, regardless of ability or device.

I started coding at 16, building small games and websites for fun. That curiosity grew into a career spanning startups, agencies, and freelance work. Today I specialise in React-based front-ends, Node.js APIs, and inclusive design systems.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me contributing to open-source accessibility projects, hiking trails around the Bay Area, or experimenting with generative art.

Values & Approach

  • Accessibility first – WCAG compliance isn't a checkbox; it's a mindset.
  • Performance matters – Fast sites respect users' time and data.
  • Clean code – Readable, maintainable, well-documented.
  • Continuous learning – The web evolves; so do I.
